Our Project:

In the future, everything will be connected with sensors as an Internet of Things (IOT). IOT empowers citizens to innovate with the data collected around them by making the data easily accessible on the Internet. In this project, sensors are placed in various locations around the University of Calgary campus, and data is gathered to describe the different building environments. Focusing the data collection on Wi-Fi speed, temperature, and noise level allows users to effectively compare study spaces according to their preferences. As the data is collected in real-time, it is presented on a user-friendly website to allow students to make smart, quick decisions when selecting a study space.
